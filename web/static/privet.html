DEPARTMENTS

Dividing the company into three departments enhances focus and efficiency. Sadra Boushehri Design Studio is dedicated to creativity and collection design, reinforcing Bocaveli’s luxury image. Bocaveli handles small, high-quality projects (1-10 units), maintaining premium standards for kitchens and wardrobes. Porta Rossa oversees larger, more complex projects, prioritizing budget control, timeline adherence, and robust project management. This structure improves client satisfaction and aligns each department with specific project needs and scales.

MATERIALS

Material combination is crucial at Bocaveli as it defines the quality, aesthetics, and functionality of our designs. By carefully selecting and combining materials, we create cabinetry that is not only visually stunning but also durable and highly customized to each client's needs. Using diverse materials—such as wood, metal, glass, and stone—allows us to achieve unique textures, finishes, and contrasts that enhance the luxury feel of each project. This attention to material choice and pairing reflects Bocaveli’s commitment to craftsmanship and innovation, ensuring that every piece we create is both a functional and artistic statement in our clients' spaces.
