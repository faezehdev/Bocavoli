BRAVURA

Bocaveli began as a small design studio focused on bespoke cabinetry and interior solutions. Over two decades, it evolved into a leading brand recognized for its architectural approach to kitchens and living spaces. Partnering with European brands, Bocaveli emphasizes quality, creativity, and functionality, redefining luxury through timeless craftsmanship and innovative designs.

SAAD ABAD

Naam Gallery, located in the heart of Tehran, is a vibrant hub for contemporary art, showcasing a diverse range of works from both emerging and established artists. The gallery is dedicated to promoting innovative artistic expressions that reflect the dynamic culture and creativity of Iran. With thoughtfully curated exhibitions, Naam Gallery provides a platform for dialogue and appreciation of art in various forms. Bocaveli, as a proud sponsor of Naam Gallery, supports its mission to enrich the local art scene and foster connections between artists and the community. Together, they aim to enhance cultural engagement and inspire a new generation of art enthusiasts.
